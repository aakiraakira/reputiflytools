Common Questions

Why $1,499 for a whole quarter when other agencies charge $2,500+ per month?

Three reasons. First, we don't bill a percentage of your ad spend — that's where most agencies make the bulk of their fee. Second, our Nexus API automates the conversion-tracking work other agencies bill hourly. Third, we only work with service businesses, so we re-use the same proven account structures rather than rebuilding from scratch each time. Specialisation lets us charge less while delivering more.

How much should I spend on Google Ads each month?

For most Singapore service businesses, SGD 1,500–3,000 per month is a healthy starting media budget — enough for Google's AI to leave the learning phase and start optimizing. Your first Account Health report includes a recommended spend tied to your service category, average CPC, and competition. You pay Google directly for clicks; we never touch the money.

Why phrase and exact match only? Isn't broad match better for reach?

Broad match gives you reach. Reach is not the goal — qualified clicks are. WordStream and Google's own studies show 40–60% of broad-match impressions are unrelated to the advertised offer. Phrase and exact match keep every click connected to actual buyer intent, which means a lower cost per real customer even though your impression count looks smaller on a dashboard.

What's the Nexus API and why does it matter?

Nexus is our proprietary tracking layer that connects your form submissions, phone calls, and bookings back to the exact keyword, ad, and landing page that drove them. Standard Google Ads tracking counts a form fill as a "conversion" — even if the lead never picked up the phone. Nexus tracks the lead through the funnel so you only count revenue that actually arrived. It's the difference between "we got 50 leads" and "we got 12 paying customers".

How long until I see results?

Week 1 — three diagnostic reports delivered. Week 2 — campaigns built and Nexus tracking installed. Week 3 — campaigns go live; Google's AI enters a learning phase (typically 10–14 days) where conversion costs are uneven. From week 4 onward, the algorithm stabilises and bidding starts targeting your best-converting keyword patterns. Most service business clients see their first attributed bookings within 14–21 days of launch.

Do you only work with service businesses?

Yes. Dental clinics, law firms, aesthetic spas, home renovation contractors, aircon servicing, cleaning, tuition, financial advisory, medical practices, etc. We decline e-commerce, B2B SaaS, and large enterprise accounts because the keyword research, ad copy, and conversion logic are fundamentally different. Staying narrow is how we deliver more for less.

What happens at the end of the quarter?

You get a full performance report showing which keywords drove paying customers, your cost per booked customer, the negative-keyword bank we built, and the next quarter's recommended changes. You can renew for another quarter at the same SGD 1,499 fee, scale your media budget based on the data, or take the account in-house — your campaigns are yours either way. No clawback, no lock-in.

Do you guarantee a specific ROAS?

No. Anyone who guarantees a specific ROAS on Google Ads is either lying or padding the bill. What we guarantee is the work — the three diagnostic reports, the campaign build, the Nexus tracking, the weekly optimisation. If the reports don't reveal three concrete things wrong with your current setup, or if we can't show you keyword-to-customer attribution by week 4, we refund the quarter.

Can I do this myself?

Yes — Google Ads is learnable. Most service business owners try, get burned by broad match for 3–6 months, then call an agency. The honest question is whether your time is worth more in your business or inside the Google Ads UI. If you'd rather treat ads as a system that runs in the background, that's what this package is for.
