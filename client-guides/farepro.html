Operation Manual

1. How Access Works

The maid database is protected so only registered users can see it. The system handles this automatically:

Step 1
User Visits

Visitor lands on
/maid-search/

Step 2
System Check

Checks if they are logged in.

Not Logged In

They see a restricted screen asking them to Sign Up or Log In.

Logged In

They get full access to the database, search filters, and profile details.

2. Managing Listings

Adding a New Maid

1

Go to WordPress Dashboard.

Hover over Maids > Click Add New Maid.

2

Enter a Title.

You can use the name or reference ID here (e.g., "Maid Ref 101").

3

Upload Photo

Important: Upload the photo in the "Featured Image" box on the right sidebar. Do not paste it into the main text box.

4

Fill in Details.

Scroll down to the custom fields. Enter Name, Nationality, Age, etc. The WhatsApp button is created automatically from this data.

5

Click Publish.

Deleting a Maid

X

To remove a listing:

  1. Hover over Maids > Click All Maids.
  2. Hover over the listing you want to remove.
  3. Click Trash.

This removes it from the search portal immediately.

3. Managing Users

Monitor who has signed up or remove accounts if needed.

View Signups

  • 1. Go to WordPress Dashboard.
  • 2. Click Users > All Users.
  • 3. Look for the Subscriber role. These are your leads.

Remove Users

To delete an account:

  • 1. Hover over the username.
  • 2. Click the red Delete link.
  • 3. Confirm deletion.
