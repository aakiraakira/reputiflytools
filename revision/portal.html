Three quick steps to send us your changes.

General Comments

Notes, images or PDFs for the whole page. Optional.

Use this for anything that applies to the whole page rather than one section, for example "update all the project photos below", or files to share such as a portfolio or a PDF. Drop images or PDFs here and add a quick note so we know what to do with them. Most revisions don't need this, it's fine to leave blank.

Good Examples
  • "Use these photos for the projects below."
  • "Capitalize all the headings on this page."
  • "Here is our brochure (PDF) for reference."
Better In A Section
  • "Change the hero image." (do that in the Hero section)
  • "Fix the spacing in the contact form."
