Common Questions

What is SEO, and why do I need it?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the process of improving your website so it shows up higher on Google when people search for what you sell. Higher rankings mean more visitors, more leads, and more sales without paying for ads.

Will my website rank on Google after launch?

New websites enter a "sandbox" period. While we optimize immediately, significant rankings typically start appearing within 3-6 months as Google builds trust in your domain.

How long will it take to see results?

SEO is a long-term compounder. You will see technical improvements in month 1, keyword movements in months 2-3, and significant traffic growth by month 6.
