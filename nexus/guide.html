Every Feature Explained

Here's what your portal can do.

Dedicated Client Portal

Your own secure login at reputifly.org/nexus/client. Only you can see your data. Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile.

Real-Time Lead Capture

When someone fills out your website's contact form, it appears in your portal instantly. No delays, no manual entry.

Instant Email Alerts

You receive a formatted email within 1 second of a new lead, with full name, email, phone, and message.

Branded Auto-Reply

Every lead gets an automatic email response with your logo and branding, so they know you received their enquiry.

Lead Management

Filter leads by source, date, or keyword. Use the search bar to find any lead instantly. Add private notes to track follow-ups.

Response Time Tracking

The system automatically measures how long it takes you to respond to each lead. 78% of deals go to the first responder.

Analytics Dashboard

Visual charts showing lead volume over time, source breakdowns, contact rates, and KPI cards. Updated in real-time.

AI Insights NEW

One-click analysis powered by Google Gemini. Get key metrics, trends, warnings, and action items. Download as a branded PDF report.

CSV Import & Export

Export your leads to a spreadsheet anytime, or import leads from other sources via CSV upload.

Weekly Email Reports

Every Monday you receive a performance summary with lead volume, contact rates, and response times for the past week.

Smart Review Filtering SMART

Auto-reply emails include a star rating. Customers who rate 4-5 stars get redirected to your Google Reviews page. 3 stars or below go to a private feedback form instead, so you can handle issues before they go public.

Spam & Blacklist Protection

Automatic filtering blocks spam submissions and bad-actor domains with configurable rate limits.
