Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about shipping with Cargo Cove.

How do I track my shipment?

You can track your shipment in real-time using our dashboard. Simply enter your reference number in the 'Track Order' section or log in to your account for a detailed map view.

What regions do you cover?

We provide global logistics coverage, with primary hubs in North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific. Our partner network extends to over 150 countries.

Do you offer customs clearance?

Yes, all our international shipping quotes include optional customs brokerage services. Our team handles documentation and compliance for you.

How fast is the air cargo service?

Our standard air cargo service delivers within 3-5 business days globally. We also offer an express "Next Flight Out" service for critical shipments.
