About Green City Air-Con

Your Trusted Partner for
Home Cooling Solutions.

Established in 2009, Green City Air-Con & Engineering Services has been the backbone of comfort for thousands of Singaporean homes. Operating from No 322C Changi Road, we bring over a decade of specialized expertise exclusively to residential units.

Whether it's routine servicing, intensive chemical overhauls, parts replacement, or entirely new installations, we handle it all with military precision. We don't just clean filters; we engineer longevity into your cooling systems.

Our promise is simple: Prompt response, transparent zero-hidden-fee pricing, and meticulous workmanship that guarantees your peace of mind.
