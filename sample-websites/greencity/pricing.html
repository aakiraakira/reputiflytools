Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I service my air-con?? expand_more

Every 3–4 months for normal usage.

When do I need a chemical wash? expand_more

When airflow is weak or mild smell starts.

When do I need a chemical overhaul? expand_more

If your AC is leaking, heavily clogged, or not cold even after basic service.

Why is my air-con leaking water? expand_more

Usually due to a clogged drain pipe or dirty coil.
