About Us

Established in 2009, Green City Air-Con & Engineering Services has been serving Singapore homeowners with professional, reliable and value-driven residential air-conditioning solutions. Located at No 322C Changi Road #02-01, Singapore 419797, we specialise exclusively in residential units — from routine servicing, chemical washes and parts replacements, to installations and troubleshooting. With prompt response times, transparent pricing and a team of skilled technicians, we’re committed to delivering comfort, peace of mind and top-notch workmanship for your home cooling needs.
