Air-con Services

General Air-con Servicing

Routine air-con servicing in Singapore to clean filters, coils, and drainage systems. Improves cooling performance, airflow, and extends air-con lifespan for homes and offices.

Professional air-con chemical wash and overhaul services for heavily soiled or low-cooling units. Removes deep dirt, bacteria and odours to restore cooling efficiency and hygiene.

Expert air-con installation services in Singapore for residential and commercial units. Proper mounting, piping, drainage, and safe electrical connections for long-term reliability.
