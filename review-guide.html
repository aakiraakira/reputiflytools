Privacy Protocol

How to Hide Review History

By default, anyone who clicks your profile can see every review you have ever written. To protect your privacy while still supporting the business, you must enable Restricted Profile mode (by turning off public posts).

On Mobile (iOS & Android)

  1. 1
    Open the Google Maps app.
  2. 2
    Tap your Profile Picture in the top right corner.
  3. 3
    Select Settings → Personal Content.
  4. 4
    Scroll down to "Profile Settings". Find "Show your posts on your profile" and toggle it OFF.

On Desktop (Computer)

  1. 1
    Go to Google Maps in your browser.
  2. 2
    Click the Menu (3 lines) in the top left → select Your Contributions.
  3. 3
    Click the tab that says Contribute → then Profile Settings.
  4. 4
    Find "Show your posts on your profile" and toggle it OFF.

Identity Protocol

How to Change Your User Name

Important Note

Google Reviews use your main Google Account name. Changing this will change your name across all Google services (including Gmail and YouTube).

Step 1: Access Account

Open Google on any device (App or Browser).

  • Tap your Profile Picture.
  • Select Manage your Google Account.
Step 2: Edit Name

Navigate to the personal tab.

  • Tap the Personal Info tab.
  • Tap Name.
  • Click the Pencil Icon to edit your Display Name.
  • Save Changes.
Go to My Google Account Settings
The Verification

How to Verify Your Review is Public

Why This Matters

Sometimes Google "shadow-bans" reviews (you can see it, but nobody else can). Sending a screenshot while you are logged in proves nothing. You must follow the procedure below to prove the review is truly public.

The "Incognito" Protocol

01
Go Incognito / Private Use a different browser or open an Incognito Window (Chrome) or Private Mode (Safari). You MUST NOT be logged into your Google account.
02
Search the Business Go to Google Maps or Google Search and type in the exact name of the business you reviewed.
03
Filter by "Newest" Click on the Reviews section. Click the "Sort" button and select Newest. This is crucial to finding your review quickly.
04
Capture the Proof If you see your review here while logged out, it is live.
Take a FULL screenshot. Do not crop the screen. We need to see that you are in Incognito mode.

Review Not Showing?

How to confirm your review was filtered
Check 1: Your View

When logged into your own account, the review says "Posted" and looks normal.

Check 2: Public View

When you check in Incognito Mode (as per the protocol above), the review is missing.

The Result

If you see it, but Incognito doesn't, it is not public.
Simply delete the review, wait 48 hours, and try again.
